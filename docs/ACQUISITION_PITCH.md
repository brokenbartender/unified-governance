# Acquisition Pitch: Unified Governance Layer

## What It Is
A drop-in policy-as-code and evidence engine for third-party and AI data access governance.

## Why It Matters
- Enterprises need visibility across vendors, SaaS, and AI tools.
- Compliance requirements are increasing while audit cycles are slow and manual.

## What You Get
- Enforceable policies with clear allow/deny decisions
- Evidence exports with tamper-evident signatures
- Connector SDK pattern for rapid integrations

## Buyer Fit
- GRC and compliance platforms
- AI security vendors
- Data governance suites
- Big 4 or enterprise consultancies

## Integration Story
- API-first service
- SQLite now; pluggable DB layer for production
- Minimal dependencies
